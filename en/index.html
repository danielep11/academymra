The Science of Ammonia

A way of orientation toward knowledge

The Science of Ammonia offers an initial orientation to those who feel the need for a language, a form and a direction through which to approach knowledge.

It does not present itself as a closed system, but as an initial support: it helps one understand better, distinguish with greater clarity and prepare for a gradual overcoming of paradigms.

For this reason it represents a precious point of access for those who feel the call to study and wish to begin with greater order, presence and awareness.
